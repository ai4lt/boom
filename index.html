BOOM

Beyond Only One Modality: KIT's Multimodal Multilingual Lecture Companion
Sai Koneru Fabian Retkowski Christian Huber Lukas Hilgert Seymanur Akti Enes Yavuz Ugan Alexander Waibel Jan Niehues
Karlsruhe Institute of Technology (KIT)

Abstract

BOOM is a multimodal multilingual lecture companion system developed at KIT. This project goes beyond single modality approaches to provide comprehensive support for lecture understanding and interaction across multiple languages and modalities.

Project Overview

The BOOM system integrates various modalities to enhance the lecture experience, making educational content more accessible and interactive for students across different languages and learning preferences.
